Car Maintenance Guide

Regular car maintenance is essential for vehicle longevity. This includes oil changes every 5,000 miles, tire rotations, and brake inspections.

Engine oil should be checked monthly. Low oil levels can cause serious engine damage. Always use the recommended oil grade for your vehicle.

Brake pads typically last 30,000-70,000 miles depending on driving habits. Listen for squealing sounds which indicate worn brake pads.

Tire pressure should be checked weekly. Proper inflation improves fuel efficiency and handling.
